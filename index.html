About

Hello! I'm an undergraduate senior majoring in Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Dallas (UTD), where I did research in surgical robotics and developed an indoor navigation system for the UTD campus. During this time, I was extremely grateful to be advised by these awesome professors: Ann Majewicz Fey and Ravi Prakash.

I am currently interested in exploring AI/ML for applications in robotic surgery and producing more knowledge in this field.
